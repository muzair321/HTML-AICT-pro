Dave The Minion

The cheerful, excitable two-eyed minion known for his enthusiasm and friendliness

Dave is one of Gru’s most expressive and energetic minions, always eager to help, experiment, or cause accidental chaos. He’s affectionate, curious, and surprisingly resourceful, often getting himself into trouble while trying to do the right thing. His goofy charm and childlike innocence make him a fan favorite among the minions.
