Stoick the Vast

The formidable, duty-bound Viking chief whose unshakable strength, fierce loyalty, and evolving understanding of his son redefine what true leadership and fatherhood mean in Berk.

Stoick the Vast begins as the embodiment of traditional Viking values—unyielding strength, steadfast duty, and an iron belief in protecting Berk at all costs. As chief, he carries the weight of his people with unwavering resolve, often placing responsibility above personal desires. Though initially rigid and unable to understand Hiccup’s unconventional ways, Stoick’s journey becomes one of learning, softening, and embracing change. His fierce love for his son ultimately transforms him, revealing a man capable of immense humility and growth. Stoick’s devotion, courage, and sacrifice position him not only as a powerful leader but as a deeply human father whose legacy shapes Berk long after he is gone.
