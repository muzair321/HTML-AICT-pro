Arlecchino

The cold, calculating Fourth Fatui Harbinger known as “Father” of the House of the Hearth

Arlecchino is one of the most enigmatic and intimidating figures in Genshin Impact, feared for her strict discipline and unwavering loyalty to her own ideals. As the leader of the House of the Hearth, she raises orphaned children with a harsh philosophy that shapes them into fiercely independent adults. Despite her cold exterior, she maintains a complex moral code and values genuine strength and honesty. Her dual nature—both ruthless and protective—makes her fascinating to fans, who view her as one of the most intriguing Harbingers. Arlecchino’s presence blends elegance, power, and unpredictability, making her a standout figure in the Fatui hierarchy.
