Gojo Satorou

The overwhelmingly powerful, sharp-tongued Jujutsu sorcerer whose unmatched abilities, rebellious ideals, and chaotic charm redefine what it means to be both a mentor and a living legend

Satoru Gojo stands at the center of Jujutsu Kaisen as an unstoppable force—equal parts genius, menace, and guardian. With the Limitless and Six Eyes, he towers above the entire sorcerer world, earning fear, admiration, and controversy in equal measure. Despite his casual arrogance and playful attitude, Gojo holds deep convictions: he wants to break the corrupt jujutsu system and nurture a new generation capable of surpassing him. Beneath the blindfold and jokes lies a man shaped by loneliness, responsibility, and grief—someone who hides his burdens behind a smile because he knows no one else can shoulder them. His unmatched power, magnetic personality, and tragic undertones make him one of anime’s most iconic modern characters.
