Moon Baek

A morally ambiguous mastermind who pulls strings from the shadows with unnerving calm

Moon Baek is the enigmatic antagonist of Trigger, a man whose past is shrouded in as much secrecy as his motives. Operating through calculated manipulation and psychological warfare, he stays three steps ahead of everyone, making him a constant looming threat. His chilling composure contrasts with the chaos he orchestrates, turning him into a villain both captivating and terrifying. Moon Baek isn’t evil for the sake of evil—he believes his actions serve a greater, twisted order, making his philosophy as dangerous as his power.
